Duane McKeever

Duane McKeever is an elite Irish drifter, known for his aggressive style and technical skill. Starting at 14, he quickly became a dominant force in European drifting. A multiple Drift Masters European Championship winner, McKeever recently claimed victory at the 2024 Warsaw event, showcasing his precision and adaptability. His fearless driving in high-stakes battles has made him one of the top competitors in the sport.

Our History

The Drift Masters European Championship, established in 2014, is a top-tier drifting series featuring elite drivers from Europe and beyond. Producing top talents like James Deane and Jack Shanahan. Drifting is based on controlled high-speed slides, precision, and style.
